Lets Talk About Cities!!

Urban Planning encompasses many topics with different scope, target groups and implications. Some of these have been discussed below!
Transit Oriented Development
Transit Oreited Development greatly impacts the economic development in the surrounding area.TOD is one of the best strategies to improve the urban vitality of your city, especially if you combine it with some of the downtown revitalizatiion strategies.
Community Development
Public engagement for community development is one of the most important tools for inclusive planning. They know the area much better than we do as planners and hence their opinion matters the most. Planning charrete, workshops and interactive surveys are some of the new tools that have proven successful for community development.
Streets for People
Streets for people is an idea that has been evolving through many years. Bikers and pedestrians are very important stakeholders who need to be attended and met with their needs. Public plazas, shared bike lanes and pleasant sidewalks form a perfect environment for them. Jan Gehl demonstrates some of the best practices for desiging streets for people.
